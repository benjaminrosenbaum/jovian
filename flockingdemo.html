Flocks
A simulation of bird flocking behavior with HTML5 canvas.





Play with the sliders on the left to change the parameters. Click on the canvas to disrupt the flock.

Separation ensures that the birds don't get crowded and move too close to each other. Alignment controls the level at which the birds tend to move in the same direction. Cohesion is responsible for "togetherness", it controls the level at which the birds steer towards the center of the flock.
